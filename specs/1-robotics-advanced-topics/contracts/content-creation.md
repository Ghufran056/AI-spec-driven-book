## Content Requirements

### Mandatory Sections
Each lesson must contain (in order):
1. Frontmatter with title, sidebar_position, and description
2. Learning Goals (5-7 items)
3. Introduction (50-100 words)
4. Main Theory with 8-12 subtopics (400-600 words)
5. Diagram (ASCII or detailed description)
6. Practical Example (100-200 words)
7. Summary (50-100 words)
8. Glossary (5-10 terms)
9. MCQs (5 questions with answers)

### Quality Standards
- Technical accuracy for advanced robotics concepts
- Beginner-friendly explanations
- Consistent terminology across all lessons
- Docusaurus MDX compatibility
- Alignment with Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics syllabus

### Validation Criteria
- Word count between 600-900 words per lesson
- All required sections present
- MCQs with unambiguous answers
- Proper MDX formatting
- Cross-references to earlier concepts where appropriate
